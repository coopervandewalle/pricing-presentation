Option 3

Fixed Rate Fee

Predictable flat monthly pricing that doesn't scale with revenue

📊 What is a Fixed Rate Fee?

Instead of the Variable Platform Fee (VPF) model where costs scale with your GMV, a Fixed Rate plan provides a consistent monthly fee regardless of how much revenue you process through Shopify.

VPF vs Fixed Rate Comparison

Variable Platform Fee (VPF)

  • ✓ Pay as you grow
  • ✓ Lower cost at lower volumes
  • ✗ Costs increase with revenue
  • ✗ Less predictable budgeting

Fixed Rate Fee

  • ✓ Predictable monthly costs
  • ✓ Cost stays flat as you grow
  • ✓ Better for high-growth scenarios
  • ✓ Easier budget forecasting

Growth Scenario Example

You mentioned expecting revenue growth once on the new platform. Here's how Fixed Rate protects against scaling costs:

💡 Key Insight

If you grow by $10M in the first year on Shopify, under VPF you'd pay an additional ~$1,500/month. With Fixed Rate, your costs remain unchanged—letting you reinvest those savings back into growth.

When Fixed Rate Makes Sense

  • You anticipate significant revenue growth in the near term
  • Budget predictability is important for your finance team
  • You want to decouple platform costs from business success
  • You're planning aggressive marketing or expansion initiatives
  • Historical patterns show seasonal or rapid growth periods
